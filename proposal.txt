Project Proposal: Boba Shop Simulation

My project is a simulation of a boba shop. The program will model how drink orders move through the shop over time as workers prepare and complete them. This simulation will show how the shop changes during a sequence of time periods and how different drink stations handle their orders.

The program will use an std::map as the main data structure. Each key in the map will be the name of a drink station in the shop, such as MilkTea, Matcha, FruitTea, and BrownSugar. The value for each key will be a 3-element std::array of std::lists. These three lists will represent the three stages of drink orders at that station: waiting orders, orders in progress, and completed orders.

The simulation will run for at least 25 time periods. During each time period, drinks may move from the waiting list to the in-progress list, and from the in-progress list to the completed list. 

The purpose of the simulation is to show how orders flow through the boba shop and how the number of waiting, in-progress, and completed drinks changes over time at each station.
